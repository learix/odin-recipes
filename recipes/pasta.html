Zach's standard pasta

Description

This dish is a favorite of Zach, a perfect homemade pasta that will warm the belly and feed the soul.

Ingredients

  • Dried kidney beans
  • Dried black beans
  • Dried navy beans
  • One small can of tomato paste
  • One box of chicken or beef bone broth
  • One pound of your favorite meat (chicken, beef, pork)
  • Numerous spices to your taste

Steps

  1. Put Instapot on saute mode
  2. Brown meat with combined spices
  3. Pour in broth and get the burned bits off of the bottom
  4. Add tomato paste and other ingredients (Jalapenos, Green Peppers, etc)
  5. Put Instapot on chilli mode for one hour with dried beans and let the steam have a natural release
